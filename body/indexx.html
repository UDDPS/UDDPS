Unified data platforms that share and update information in real time enable better, faster, and easier government services. Government agencies can expand their efficiency and effectiveness by separating data storage and creating a unified data base. But they must do so, of course, within the legal framework of their country

National Bureau of Investigation

The National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) is a line agency under the Department of Justice in the Philippines and serves as the premier investigative agency of the government.

NBI Clearance Requirements

  • Passport
  • PRC card.
  • UMID.
  • PSA-issued birth certificate.
  • PhilHealth ID.
  • Voter's ID
  • TIN ID.
  • Driver's license


    • nbi.gov.ph

Land Transportation Office

The Land Transportation Office is an agency of the Philippine government under the Department of Transportation responsible for all land transportation in the Philippines.

  • License and Permit
  • Vehicle Registration
  • Law Enforcement


    • lto.gov.ph

Bureau of Internal Revenue

The Bureau of Internal Revenue is an agency of Department of Finance. BIR collects more than half of the total revenues of the government. Caesar Dulay is the current Commissioner of BIR.

  • Enforce all forfeitures penalties and fines
  • Collect all national internal taxes fees and charges


    • bir.gov.ph

Philippine Statistics Authority

The Philippine Statistics Authority, abbreviated as PSA, is the central statistical authority of the Philippine government that collects, compiles, analyzes and publishes statistical information on economic, social, demographic, political affairs and general affairs of the people of the Philippines and enforces the civil registration functions in the country.

  • Birth certificates
  • Death certificates
  • Marriage certificates


    • psa.gov.ph
